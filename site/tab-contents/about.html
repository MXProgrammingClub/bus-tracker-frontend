About MX Bus Tracker

The Middlesex Bus Tracker was first conceived in 2014 when the then Deans of Students, Carmen Beaton and Dan Sheff, wanted a way to remove the crowds of students waiting for the bus in the Concord Dunkin' Donuts. For two years, a group of students worked to create a tracking device, allowing students to know exactly where the bus is at all times.

The students who worked on the Middlesex Bus Tracker are: Harry Xue `17, Luke Giacalone `17, Ted Pyne `18, and Hyun Choi `17.
